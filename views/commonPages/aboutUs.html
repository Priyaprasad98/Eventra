Who We Are

We are a dedicated community responsible for publishing regular updates about events and initiatives from different clubs and societies across the campus.

From NSS, Empyrean, XTS, and Rotaract to many more, this is your central hub to discover what’s happening, what’s next, and how to get involved.

Our goal is to keep every student informed, engaged, and connected with timely announcements, event highlights, and participation opportunities.
